Overview

Purpose

To bring together local communities of amateur birdwatchers through social media and blog-style posting.

Audience

Amateur birdwatchers in local communities. Probably older (50-70) audiences will utilize the sight the most, but the website should not be age exclusive. That being said, the plan is to keep it simple to read and understand (big text, simple layout).

Content

Home page

Welcome to our online community for birdwatching enthusiasts! Our website is designed specifically for retired individuals aged 40-70 who have a passion for observing and learning about birds.

Our platform is a place where you can connect with like-minded individuals who share your interest in birdwatching. You can share your birdwatching experiences, photos, and tips with other members of the community, as well as learn from their experiences.

We understand that birdwatching is a hobby that requires patience, knowledge, and dedication. Therefore, our website offers a variety of resources and tools that can help enhance your birdwatching experience. Here are some of the features that you can expect to find on our website:

Mobile-friendly platform: Our website is optimized for mobile devices, so you can access our platform from anywhere at any time. Whether you're at home or on the go, you can stay connected with the birdwatching community.

At our website, we are committed to creating a safe and welcoming environment for all birdwatching enthusiasts. We have a dedicated team that monitors the discussion forums and photo uploads to ensure that our community guidelines are being followed.

In addition, we understand that retirees may not be as familiar with technology as younger generations. That's why our website is designed to be user-friendly and easy to navigate, with clear instructions and support available if you need it.

We believe that birdwatching is a hobby that can bring people together, regardless of age or background. Our website is dedicated to fostering a sense of community among birdwatching enthusiasts, and we hope that you will join us on this exciting journey.

In conclusion, if you're a retired individual who loves birdwatching, our website is the perfect place to connect with other enthusiasts and enhance your birdwatching experience. With our local events calendar, birdwatching tips and tricks, social media integration, and mobile-friendly platform, we have everything you need to take your hobby to the next level. Join our community today and start exploring the world of birds!
